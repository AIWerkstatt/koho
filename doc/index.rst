Welcome to koho\ |trade|'s documentation!
=========================================

**koho** (Hawaiian word for 'to estimate') is a **Decision Forest** **C++ library**
with a `scikit-learn`_ compatible **Python interface**.

.. _`scikit-learn`: http://scikit-learn.org
